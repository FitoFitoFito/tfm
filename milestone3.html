Style Guide

Typography - Color Guide - Buttons - Panels Cards

Typography

Montserrat (Sans Serif family) is the selected typography for this website. With this typo we will transmit modernity, minimalism, and joy.

The selected fonts are: Regular, Medium, Semibold, and Bold.

Color Guide

Blue and its shades are the main color on this website. It is said that this color is often associated with tranquility and reliability, and it is also used when you want to project an image of security. Take a look at the Figma draft for additional information.

Below I present the color palette that is going to be used on the website along with their hexadecimal codes.

Buttons

There are 3 button states that communicate the status of the elements to the user: Enabled, Disabled and Hover

Panel Cards

The panels will be rectangular, its direction can be horizontal or vertical. Each panel will have a 6px border with a 25% of blur and a 15% of opacitty. The shadow will be central i.e. x = 0 and y = 0 and the color (in hexadecimal) will be #06232C.
